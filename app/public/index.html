Our Afflilate Management Process

  • Identify Goals

    Every affiliate program is unique; we want to thoroughly understand your goals for your campaign.

  • Create Impactful Content

    We strive to create the most effective and engaging content for our clients; content leads to clicks, and clicks lead to results.

  • Test

    All of our content is rigorously split-tested to discover what is working best and why.

  • Measure

    Data is continuously collected throughout the life of the campaign. This data gives us insight into who is viewing your targeted content, what they are clicking on (CTR Click Through Rate), and what needs to be done to improve those numbers.

  • Optimize

    Based on the data we collect, we create an actionable plan to improve the content and campaign to drive the best results for your brand.

  • Report

    We believe in transparency. We provide you with weekly and monthly reports throughout the life of the campaign.
